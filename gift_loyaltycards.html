Gift & Loyalty Cards
Gift and loyalty cards enable merchants to generate immediate cash for products or services which are provided in the future.
Gift and loyalty cards are also great tools for increasing sales and building brand awareness for any size business.
Gift Cards
Your Gift Card can be customized or designed for you or you can choose from many standard designs.
Your customers can use your gift card for any occasion or reason. Gift cards are also great giveaways as gifts to friends, family, co-workers and business associates.
Loyalty Cards
Loyalty Cards are great for incentivising your customers to frequent your store or location.
Here’s how it works…Your customers earn retail merchandise credit (dollar value) or points for dollars spent and can redeem them for a free item or discount towards future purchases.
Loyalty points or credits are stored on our systems, so you never have to worry about keeping track of them.
Customers love earning points or discounts, and your loyalty card will keep them coming back to your store or location!
