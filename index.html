About Us

Bell Processing Solutions has been helping businesses of all sizes accept credit cards as a proven leader and partner in the credit card processing industry.

Bell are a full-service payment processor serving tens of thousands of clients nationwide. Bell knows that accepting credit cards for payment is essential for any business, which is why we support processing solutions for all businesses and all major credit cards including Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover, as well as providing processing services for Debit, EBT, government cards, checks, and gift and loyalty cards.

Our merchants range from national, multi-lane chain stores to mom and pop retail storefronts and restaurants. We support all types of retail, restaurant, multi-lane, e-commerce, internet, mail/telephone order, business-to-business, and hospitality merchants.

Bell's processing solutions can accommodate virtually any type of credit and debit card equipment or platform. We offer the widest selection of credit card processing programs, products, and solutions, and in most cases with same day approval.
