Check Guarantee
By adding Check Guarantee services to your merchant account, you not only save on banking and accounting costs, but you can significantly reduce losses from bad checks.
By taking a complete image capture of the check at the Point of Sale, you can accept checks without any concern.
Checks are approved in seconds and if an approved check is returned from the bank unpaid, it can be submitted for reimbursement without going to the bank, leaving you time to focus on sales and not collections.
For more information and pricing please contact your service provider or Bell directly.
