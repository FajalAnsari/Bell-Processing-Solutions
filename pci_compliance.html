PCI Compliance
PCI DSS, the Payment Card Industry Data Security Standards, was created to offer customers using credit and debit cards for payment with the highest level of trust and security. These security standards apply to all companies that process, store or transmit card and related customer information. Bell is committed to maintaining our industries highest standards of PCI DSS Compliance.
The Payment Card Industry mandates that all merchants must be certified under these Data Security Standards. However, many merchants have found implementing PCI DSS requirements confusing. As a result, Bell has made the certification process simple with an easy questionnaire form that walks you through the process.
