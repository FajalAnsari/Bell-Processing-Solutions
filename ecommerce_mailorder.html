E-Commerce and Mail Order Merchants

Processing credit cards over the Internet is one of the fastest growing segments of transactions in our Industry today.


Bell offers the ability to integrate payment processing directly into your virtual store or website. We also offer many different options to accept credit cards including the use of a virtual terminal that acts as a credit card terminal online.


Bell’s consulting approach with each merchant enables us to integrate a “Customer Tailored” processing solution with the following advantages and standards:

  • The ability to accept and process credit card payments “real time”
  • Acceptance of all major credit cards
  • Process credit card transactions from orders received offline
  • Issue credits
  • Void transactions
  • View your transactions online
  • Integrate into QuickBooks
